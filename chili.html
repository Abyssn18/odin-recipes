Chili Recipe

Description

This is a delicious chili recipe using basic ingredients you can find laying around your kitchen. It will most likely take you around and hour and a half of time to make this dish, so have some time available.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground round
  • 5 guajillo peppers
  • 4 ancho chiles
  • 2 tbs of cumin
  • 1 tbs of dried oregano
  • 1 tbs of brown sugar
  • 1 tbs of black pepper
  • 1 large white onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 cups of beef broth
  • 230ml of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of finely diced cilantro
  • 440ml can of kidney beans
  • 440ml can of black beans

Steps

    Boil the peppers until soft
    Blend the peppers with all spices, sugar, herbs, garlic, and onion until you get a paste
    Add the pepper water if it's too dry
    Brown the meat in a pan
    Mix in the tomato paste to cook for one minute
    Pour the meat and tomato paste mixture into an oven safe pot
    Add the beef broth and pepper paste
    Let it sit for one hour
    add the beans once 45 minutes of that hour has passed
    take it out, and enjoy
