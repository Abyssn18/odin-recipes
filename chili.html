Chili Recipe

Description

This is a delicious chili recipe using basic ingredients you can find laying around your kitchen. It will most likely take you around and hour and a half of time to make this dish, so have some time available.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground round
  • 5 guajillo peppers
  • 4 ancho chiles
  • 2 tbs of cumin
  • 1 tbs of dried oregano
  • 1 tbs of brown sugar
  • 1 tbs of black pepper
  • 1 large white onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 cups of beef broth
  • 230ml of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of finely diced cilantro
  • 440ml can of kidney beans
  • 440ml can of black beans

Steps

  1. Boil the peppers until soft
  2. Blend the peppers with all spices, sugar, herbs, garlic, and onion until you get a paste
  3. Add the pepper water if it's too dry
  4. Brown the meat in a pan
  5. Mix in the tomato paste to cook for one minute
  6. Pour the meat and tomato paste mixture into an oven safe pot
  7. Add the beef broth and pepper paste
  8. Let it sit for one hour
  9. add the beans once 45 minutes of that hour has passed
  10. take it out, and enjoy
