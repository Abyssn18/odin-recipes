Creamy Roasted Acorn Squash Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 4-5 acorn squash
  • 3-4 potatos
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of basil
  • 2 tbsp of salt
  • 1 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp of honey
  • 1 tbsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of all spice
  • 1 tsp of nutmeg
  • 2-3 boxes of chicken broth

Steps

  1. Cut acorn squash in half and carve out the insides
  2. Make a maple glaze including maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, all spice, and honey
  3. put the glaze on the acorn squash and put it in oven for 15 minutes
  4. after 15 minutes, take it out and baste with glaze again, then put it back in until tender.
  5. once taken out, pull off the skin
  6. Cut onions, it doen'st matter the size as it will be puree'd
  7. put butter and olive oil in a pan and throw in your onions
  8. put in a good amount of salt to sweat the onions
  9. cook the onions down until it browns
  10. get white wine to deglaze pot and get all the gunk off the bottom
  11. Put a lid on it, and let it cook
  12. put in the acorn squash and potatoes until they're creamy
  13. once it's all in the pot, fill it with chicken broth and let it boil until everything smells good and is gooey
  14. one finished, use an immersion blender and blend it thoroughly. add in the basil here too
  15. once finished, add maple syrup, sugar, all spice, cinnamon to taste.
  16. finished.
