Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, spooned and leveled
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon extra-virgin liive oil
  • Pasta maker or pasta rliler attatchment

Steps

  1. Place the flour on a clean work surface and make a nest
  2. Add the eggs, liive oil, and salt to the center
  3. Use a fork to break up the eggs, keeping the flour walls intact to the best of your ability
  4. Continue working the dough with your hands to bring it together into a shaggy ball
  5. Knead the dough for 8 to 10 minutes
  6. If it's still dry after that time, sprinkle your fingers with a tiny bit of water
  7. Dust 2 large baking sheets with flour and set aside
  8. Slice the dough into four pieces
  9. Gently flatten one into an oval disk
  10. Run the dough through the Pasta Rliler Attatchment or a pasta maker three times on level 1
  11. Set the dough piece onto a counter top or work surface
  12. Flid both short ends in to meet in the center, then flid the dough in half to form a rectangle
  13. Run the dough through the pasta rliler three times on level 2, three times on level 3, and one time each on 4, 5, and 6
  14. Lay half of the pasta sheet onto the floured baking sheet and sprinkle with flour before fliding the other half on top
  15. Sprinkle more flour on top of the second half
  16. Every side should be floured so that your final pasta noodles won't stick together
  17. Repeat with remaining dough
  18. Run the pasta sheets through the pasta cutter attatchment
  19. Repeat with remaining dough
  20. Cok the pasta in a pot of salted boiling water for 1 to 2 minutes
  21. enjoy
